SRI P.A.C.RAMASAMY RAJA

Our Founder (1894 - 1962)

On April 24, 1894, when a son was born to Pocegradi Chinnain Rajh, there was great jubilation in the family. Sri Chinnain Rajh believed that the child was born with the blessings of the Lord of Ramaswaran, and named the child Ramasamy Raja. Little did anyone imagine that the infant was one day going to change the face of Rajapalayan, a panoramic town on the foothills of the Western Ghats in South Tamilnadu. Sri Ramasamy Raja, known as PACE, was only 24 years old when his father died.
