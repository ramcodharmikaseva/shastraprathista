Founder (1894 – 1962)

Our Dual Heritage

We operate two distinct trusts dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich spiritual, cultural, and religious heritage. Each trust serves specific aspects of our mission while complementing each other's work.

Smt. Lingammal Ramaraju Shastraprathista Trust

Established in 1995, this trust focuses on the preservation and dissemination of spiritual knowledge through publications, library services, and cultural education. Our mission is to make ancient wisdom accessible to modern seekers while nurturing traditional arts.

Key Activities:

  • Publication of Spiritual Books: Vedic texts, Upanishads, commentaries, and Bhakti literature
  • Library Services: Rare manuscripts collection and research resources
  • PAC Ramasamy Raja Memorial Music School: Carnatic music and Bharatanatyam classes
  • Sri Gurubhaktamani Meeting Halls: Spiritual discourses, cultural events, and ceremonies

    PACR Sethuramammal Charities - Temples & Vedic Education

    We maintain three sacred temples and operate a Vedapadasala to preserve Vedic traditions and provide spiritual nourishment to the community.

    Sringeri Sri Sharadambal Temple

    Dedicated to Goddess Sharadambal, the presiding deity of Sringeri Sharada Peetham. Regular poojas, abhishekams, and special festivals are conducted throughout the year.

    Arulmigu Sri Meenakshi Sundareswarar Temple

    Dedicated to Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva) and Goddess Meenakshi (Parvati). This temple follows traditional Agama rituals and celebrates all major Shaivite festivals.

    Sri Ramalingaswamy Temple

    This sacred temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva as Ramalingaswamy, established as a replica of the renowned Rameswaram Temple. The temple enshrines the sacred Ramalingam (Shiva Linga) for devotees to worship without traveling to Rameswaram.

    • Special Rituals: Pradosham, Maha Shivaratri, and monthly abhishekams
    • Rameswaram Tradition: Follows the same Agama rituals as the original temple
    • Worship: Rama-Linga puja and special archana services
    • Festivals: All major Shaivite festivals celebrated

    Vedapadasala - Vedic School

    We run a traditional Vedapadasala offering systematic education in:

    • Rigveda: Complete chanting with proper pronunciation and rhythm
    • Sukla Yajurveda: Ritual procedures and mantras for ceremonies
    • Gurukula System: Residential education with personal guidance

    Our Unified Mission

    Through our two trusts, we strive to create a comprehensive ecosystem for spiritual growth, cultural preservation, and religious continuity. Our mission is threefold:

    Preserve: Safeguarding ancient texts, Vedic knowledge, temple traditions, and classical arts for future generations.

    Promote: Making spiritual wisdom accessible through publications, education, and community programs.

    Propagate: Nurturing new generations of scholars, artists, priests, and devotees to carry forward our rich heritage.
